The Soil Factory, a project of Weave Community, is a platform for building networks, collaborations, and experiments along the art/science/sustainability interface.

It brings together artists, students, scientists, farmers, and community members to share work, ask questions, model solutions, and discuss the most important issues of our day.

The Soil Factory is located in Ithaca, NY, and within its warehouse and meadow, everyone is invited to come, be inspired, and join the conversation.

For more information, see [The Soil Factory](http://thesoilfactory.org) website.
